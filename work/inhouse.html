InHouse

Designing the ultimate shopping platform for hosts looking to elevate their Airbnb.

  • Content Strategy
  • UX Design
  • UI Motion
  • Visual Design
  • Prototyping

InHouse offers an exclusive shopping experience, featuring premium products designed specifically with hosts in mind.

Jae Yoon Studio was selected by InHouse to design an impressionable and engaging user experience for their very first online presence.

Starting off strong

As the first impression for online visitors, the homepage of InHouse needed to convey the brand visually while highlighting its key features.

We placed a strong emphasis on content strategy to ensure that the value propositions were presented in the most effective order and with the appropriate level of emphasis, all while maintaining a visually cohesive and on-brand look and feel.

Central hub for hosts

The InHouse digital platform is more than just a shopping site for Airbnb hosts – it's a central hub that hosts can rely on for all their furnishing needs.

So, we placed a strong emphasis on a seamless and delightful user experience throughout all screens of the platform, including subscription management.

InHouse, from anywhere

With many of the users being busy hosts who may need to manage their space on the go, we made it a priority to provide a mobile experience for the platform that was just as seamless as the desktop version.

Shopping portal for guests

Our partnership with InHouse extended beyond creating a shopping hub for hosts.

We also developed a dedicated platform for guests to shop for the premium items placed in their Airbnb. With this platform, guests can easily find and purchase the items they like at their stay to get them delivered directly to their home.
