Transforming the W Hotel website to exude its vibrant energy.

  • Visual Design
  • Design System
  • UI Motion
  • Prototyping

W Hotels is a luxury hotel brand with a reputation for hosting fun-filled events and offering exceptional guest experiences.

Their challenge was to revamp their website with a focus on their celebratory culture to attract a younger and a more diverse audience.

For the first design review round, we presented a range of options for the team at W Hotels to feedback on.

While ensuring ease-of-use was fundamental, we also aimed to visually capture the vibrant energy of W Hotels.

Function first, form first.

Every single detail is important in good e-commerce web design (and in anything else that's good, really.)

That's why we paid close attention to the details on every single element of the design, from the free shipping tout element, to the money-back guarantee lockup illustration.

Given the prevalence of mobile usage among Current's audience, we made sure that the website was not only functional and visually appealing on mobile, but also conveyed the same level of storytelling and brand identity as on desktop.
