R+Co

Preserving R+Co's exciting brand and personality while optimizing for conversion.

  • Visual Design
  • Design System
  • UI Motion
  • Prototyping

R+Co is a New York-based hair care brand known for its innovative, high-quality products and vibrant, edgy brand image.

The challenge they faced was to redesign the R+Co website while preserving its unique identity, all while improving user experience and maximizing conversions.

We challenged ourselves to make the account dashboard have a design that is just as captivating as the main website.

This ensures users have a consistent on-brand experience throughout their entire shopping journey.

Of course, mobile optimization is always top of mind. By combining established mobile UI patterns and the R+Co brand, we were able to design visually striking mobile designs that is easy-to-use.
