Wyndham Hotels

Redesigning a leading hotel chain's online booking experience

  • UX Design
  • Visual Design
  • Design System
  • UI Motion
  • Prototyping

Wyndham Hotels wanted to redesign their website and create a seamless booking experience for their customers.

The goal was to increase conversions and improve the overall user experience on both desktop and mobile platforms.

Collaborating closely with Wyndham Hotel's design team, we created a more intutitive booking experience while also creating a new look and feel that was modern, clean, and aligned with Wyndham's brand values.

With data showing that more and more users are booking hotels on their mobile devices, it was crucial that the mobile experience was just as seamless as on desktop.

So, we designed a mobile experience that was both visually appealing and easy to navigate, ensuring that users could book their next Wyndham Hotel stay with ease, whether they were at home or on the go.
