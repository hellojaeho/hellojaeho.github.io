Bibi

Translating musical diversity and personality into an album identity concept

  • Brand Identity
  • Web Design

The true mark of an artist lies in their ability to channel multiple facets of their personality into their creative work, often resulting in a unique and distinct style for each album.

As it relates to design, the challenge is to capture the dynamic range of emotions and attitudes conveyed through the music, and distill it into a cohesive and compelling identity.

This identity concept was crafted with the intention of encapsulating the duality of Bibi's artistic vision - fierce and unapologetic, yet beautiful.
