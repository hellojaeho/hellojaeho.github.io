Ready Set Rocket

Empowering cancer patients & survivors

  • Brand Design
  • Visual Design
  • Design System
  • UI Motion
  • Prototyping

Founder of SurvivorNet, Steve Alperin, aimed to create a platform that provided support, resources, and community for cancer patients and survivors.

His mission was to empower patients with knowledge and connect them with vetted doctors and professionals.

Our collaborative efforts resulted in a digital platform that uses video content as the primary tool to help cancer patients better understand their diagnosis and treatment options.

Our design process resulted in a design system of templates and components that empowered SurvivorNet's team to develop and maintain new pages as the company grew.

Recognition

The SurvivorNet website was a resounding success, quickly becoming the top destination for verified cancer information.

The work earned industry recognition, including a Webby, and a feature on the renowned morning show, Good Morning America on ABC.
