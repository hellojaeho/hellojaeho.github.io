Nike, Stink Studios

Concepting for Nike's first-ever digital celebration of Air Max Day Worldwide.

  • Concepts
  • UX Design
  • UI Motion
  • Visual Design
  • Prototyping

Nike's annual Air Max Day event was not possible in 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

To keep the fan's excitement and momentum intact, Nike needed a virtual online rendition of the celebration.

Nike selected Stink Studios as their partner for the initiative, and Stink Studios engaged Jae Yoon Studio to design concepts, UX wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes.

Concept Exploration

Jae Yoon Studio created initial concepts to visually articulate the potential of the virtual Air Max Day event and generate excitement among stakeholders.

The designs were then presented to Nike's decision-makers to successfully secure investment and budget approval.

Emulating travel

The foundational structures were designed so that the user can experience different types of content, including video, in different "cities" around the world.

The right balance had to be met so that there is a "travelling" experience while making it easy to consume rich types of content.

Applying art direction

Once the UX blueprints were finished, we worked together with Nike Art Director, Laurence Jaccottet, to help visually bring the excitement and spirit of Air Max Day celebration, elevating the user experience to new heights.

Together, we worked to bring the essence of the event to life and create an engaging digital experience for Nike Air Max fans.

Collaborative Success

The digital Air Max Day event was a massive undertaking by Stink Studios, its partners and Nike. The end result was a resounding success, featuring over a hundred pieces of original content, including an exclusive contribution from Travis Scott.

This project won The Bronze Pencil from The One Show, the world's most prestigious award show.
