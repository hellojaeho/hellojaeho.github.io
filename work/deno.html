Deno

Designing an elegant and user-friendly web deployment experience.

  • Content Strategy
  • UX Design
  • UI Motion
  • Visual Design
  • Prototyping

Deno approached Jae Yoon Studio to revamp and revitalize their initial product, which lacked a professional feel and was hindered by a poor user experience, causing developers to be hestitant of using the platform.

While Deno already had a beloved brand with whimsical dinosaur illustrations, the product and marketing website did not effectively communicate this playful personality, leading to missed opportunities.

Jae Yoon Studio set out to showcase Deno's playful personality while also establishing them as a reliable and trustworthy deployment platform.
