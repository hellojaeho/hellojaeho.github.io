Deem, Fantasy Interactive

Transforming travel planning into a shared journey that's as enjoyable as the trip itself.

  • Concepts
  • UI Motion
  • Visual Design
  • Prototyping

Planning a group trip can be a challenging task, as everyone's preferences and requirements need to be considered.

The goal is to simplify the process by providing a user-friendly and modern platform for sharing trip ideas, as well as potential flights and hotels.

The product is designed to be both functional and fun, providing a modern and intuitive platform that enables group members to collaborate seamlessly and enjoyably as they plan their journey together.

User-friendly and inviting

Ease-of-use is of paramount importance, but it is of equal importance that the app doesn't feel like a HR platform, as the designs are meant to promote fun and camaraderie while planning with your friends.

By incorporating the right amount of vibrant colors and playful illustrations, we infuse brightness and positive energy to the users' planning process.

By striking the perfect balance between ease-of-use and a fun, engaging experience, our app ensures that group travel planning is both productive and enjoyable for all involved.
